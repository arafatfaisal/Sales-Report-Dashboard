# 📊 Executive Sales Report Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🧾 Overview
This project presents an **Executive Sales Report Dashboard built in Power BI** to provide business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, and key operational metrics.

The dashboard enables management to monitor revenue trends, identify top-performing products and regions, track KPIs, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall sales performance  
- Track revenue and profitability trends  
- Analyze sales by product, category, and region  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming segments  
- Support executive-level reporting and strategic planning  

---


---

## 📈 Key Features

### 📌 Executive KPI Summary
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin  
- Number of Orders  
- Year-over-Year Growth  

### 📊 Sales Analysis
- Monthly and yearly sales trends  
- Revenue comparison across time periods  
- Growth analysis  

### 🛍️ Product Performance
- Top-selling products  
- Product category analysis  
- Contribution to total revenue  

### 🌍 Regional Insights
- Sales by region  
- Geographic performance comparison  
- Regional growth opportunities  

### 🔎 Interactive Filtering
- Date filters  
- Product filters  
- Regional filters  
- Dynamic drill-down analysis
